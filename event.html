Details

Accommodation

For those wanting to stay with us at Milton Park, the venue can sleep 80 people so there's plenty of room for everyone!

The discount is 15% off our Bed & Breakfast rate and applicable to our Deluxe Garden Rooms. Please note this code does not work on any other offers available. Guests can book via these URL links from today right through to 7 days prior to the wedding date, all bookings are subject to availability. The access code is pre-populated in the URL link and guests just need to select their stay dates and then proceed with the booking process. A 7-day cancellation policy and deposit policy are attached to all wedding bookings.

Transport

No transportation is being organised for those not staying on site. There is parking available at Milton Park but please car pool where possible to limit vehicles.

Dress Code

Anything from cocktail to formal. We want you to feel your best, so wear what you're comfortable in! It can get cold in Bowral in September, so make sure to bring a coat or dress accordingly.

Kids

We truly love your kids, but this will be an adults only event - we want you to have fun and enjoy yourselves!

Gifts

Having lived together for over a year, we have everything we could need for our home. If you would like to give us a gift, we would truly appreciate a contribution to our wishing well.

Wet weather plan

Hopefully there's no rain... if it is lightly raining we are likely to continue with our outdoor ceremony, for heavier rain we will relocate to the Conservatory. It is September though, so bringing along an umbrella may be wise!

Dietary requirements

Please let us know when RSVPing - we will ensure there are vegetarian and vegan options, as well as addressing any other allergies
