Finer Details

Transport

Good news: you don’t need to trek anywhere to meet the coach. It will pick you up directly from your accommodation and drop you right back there at the end of the night. Door-to-door service! 🚍✨

We’ll only have around 40 people on board, so it won’t be a never-ending loop of stops as the coach makes its way towards Bendooley. Once we know where everyone’s staying, we’ll share your exact pick-up time.

We really encourage you to use the coach so everyone can relax, have a drink (or three), and we feel it’s worthwhile organising it for the whole gang, not just a few. If you’d like to use the service, just tick the option on your RSVP and pop in your accommodation address.

If you’d prefer to drive, there is plenty of safe, secure parking at The Stables. You’re also welcome to leave your car overnight if you decide to taxi or Uber home. Just keep in mind that rideshares in the Highlands are limited (though apparently there are now at least four Uber drivers in the area—progress!).

Dress Code

Please dress in formal attire (slight lean towards Cocktail is fine too). We’d love everyone to feel polished and celebratory, while still being true to themselves and comfortable.

October in Berrima is usually mild, but the evenings can be cool, so we suggest bringing a coat or wrap just in case.

For those who like a little inspiration, we’ve created a Pinterest board with some style ideas. You can check it out [here]!

Kids

We absolutely love your kids, but please note this will be an adults-only event. We want everyone to have fun and enjoy themselves to the fullest!

Gifts

Having lived together for nearly two years, we’re lucky to have most of what we need for our home. If you'd like to give us a gift, we would be truly grateful for a contribution to our wishing well.

Dietary requirements

When RSVPing, please let us know if you have any dietary requirements. We’ll be sure to provide vegetarian and vegan options, as well as accommodate any allergies.
