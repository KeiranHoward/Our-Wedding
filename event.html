Finer Details

Accommodation

For those wishing to stay with us at Milton Park, there’s plenty of room! The venue can accommodate up to 80 guests.

Milton Park has generously offered a 15% discount off their Bed & Breakfast rate for Deluxe Garden Rooms, which we’re excited to pass on to our guests. Please note that this offer is not applicable to any other promotions available at the venue.

Guests can book through the provided URL link, available from today until 7 days before the wedding. The access code is pre-populated in the link—simply select your stay dates and proceed with the booking. All bookings are subject to availability, and a 7-day cancellation and deposit policy applies to all wedding reservations.

If you’re looking for more options, there’s plenty of accommodation around Bowral as well.

Transport

Currently, no transportation is being organised for guests not staying on-site. There is parking available at Milton Park, but we kindly ask that you carpool where possible to help limit the number of vehicles. While we don’t have additional transportation plans at this stage, if we find that many guests are booking accommodation at other venues in Bowral, we’ll look into arranging something to assist with travel.

Dress Code

Anything from cocktail to formal wear attire. We want you to feel your best, so please wear what you’re comfortable in! While it's Spring, Bowral can get quite chilly in September, so be sure to bring a coat or dress warmly.

For those who like a little inspiration, we’ve created a Pinterest board with some style ideas. You can check it out [here]!

Kids

We absolutely love your kids, but please note this will be an adults-only event. We want everyone to have fun and enjoy themselves to the fullest!

Gifts

Having lived together for nearly two years, we’re lucky to have most of what we need for our home. If you'd like to give us a gift, we would be truly grateful for a contribution to our wishing well.

Wet weather plan

Fingers crossed for sunshine! If it's lightly raining, we’re likely to go ahead with our outdoor ceremony. However, if there’s heavier rain, we’ll relocate to the Conservatory. Since it’s September, we recommend bringing an umbrella just in case!

Dietary requirements

When RSVPing, please let us know if you have any dietary requirements. We’ll be sure to provide vegetarian and vegan options, as well as accommodate any allergies.
